MUNICIPAL PORTFOLIO


VISION

Make Suame to become the centre of excellence and the model municipality by 2024

MISSION

To create value and make the difference as education is made accessible and useful to all.
To enable the learner and the school community to realized their full potential through quality management and collaboration.

MANDATE

SMED is responsible for the implementation of approved national pre-tertiary educational policies and programs to ensure that all Ghanaian children of school-going age irrespective of;
tribe, gender, disability, religious and political affiliations are provided with inclusive and equitable quality formal education.
